# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want DayPilot to be a friendly, useful place to collaborate. Anyone is welcome to contribute, file issues, or ask questions — regardless of background, experience level, or platform of choice.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Critique ideas, not people
- Help newcomers when you can

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Sharing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

If someone is acting in a way that violates this, email **rahmonberdivahabov@gmail.com** with the details. Reports are confidential. The maintainer will review and respond — outcomes range from a private conversation to a permanent ban from the project's spaces.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any official communication channel.
